Privacy Policy for YKS Net Takip

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Net Takip ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how that information is used, and the choices available to you when using the app.

1. Information We Process

Net Takip is designed to help users track YKS study progress, exam results, and analytics.

Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be processed:

  • Profile information you enter in the app, such as your name, target score, target university, target department, exam year, and study preferences
  • Exam and analytics data you create in the app, including TYT, AYT, and branch exam records, subject results, total net scores, and study-related progress data
  • Subscription status and purchase-related information used to determine whether premium features are active
  • Technical identifiers required for subscription functionality, such as an app-specific user ID used with RevenueCat

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Most user data in Net Takip is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This includes profile data, exam records, goals, and general app state.

We do not currently operate a general-purpose backend that stores your study data remotely for normal app usage.

3. In-App Purchases and RevenueCat

Net Takip uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and premium entitlements.

When subscription-related features are used, certain purchase and account-related data may be processed by RevenueCat and the relevant app store provider, such as Apple or Google, in order to:

  • display available subscription plans
  • process purchases
  • restore previous purchases
  • verify premium entitlement status

For this purpose, the app may send an app-specific user identifier and subscription-related metadata to RevenueCat.

RevenueCat's privacy practices are described in its own privacy documentation:

  • https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple's and Google's store systems may also process purchase-related information under their own privacy terms.

4. How We Use Information

We use processed information to:

  • provide the core functionality of the app
  • save and display your exam and study records
  • generate analytics and progress views
  • enable premium features
  • verify and restore subscription status
  • improve app reliability and maintain service functionality

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your study records with advertisers.

Limited data may be shared with third-party service providers only where necessary to operate app functionality, such as subscription infrastructure through RevenueCat and the relevant app store provider.

6. Tracking and Advertising

Net Takip is not designed as an advertising app and does not use your data for third-party advertising based on the current app version.

7. Data Retention

Because most app data is stored locally on your device, it generally remains on your device until you delete it, remove it through app controls, reset the app's data, or uninstall the app.

Subscription-related records processed by third-party providers may be retained by those providers according to their own legal and operational retention policies.

8. Your Choices

You can:

  • edit your profile information inside the app
  • delete your locally stored app data by using in-app reset options
  • uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device
  • choose whether to purchase premium features

If you do not want subscription-related information to be processed, do not initiate or restore an in-app purchase.

9. Children's Privacy

Net Takip is intended for students and general educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe data has been provided inappropriately, please contact us through the developer contact information listed on the app's product page.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce privacy and security risks. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Use

If you use Net Takip from outside your home country, your information may be processed by third-party services in other jurisdictions as required for app functionality, purchases, and subscription management.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, the updated version will be reflected in the app listing or other appropriate locations, along with a revised effective date.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the developer contact details provided on the App Store or Play Store listing for Net Takip.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of March 28, 2026.
